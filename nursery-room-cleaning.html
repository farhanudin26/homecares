Nursery Room Cleaning

Did you know that the first month for a newborn baby is very crucial? This is because it is the most important time for their rapid growth and development. Newborns have a weaker immune system; hence it is important that the environment they will be living in is hygienically safe and comfortable.

Why should you choose our Nursery Room Cleaning service?
As strong advocates for a safe and hygienic environment for the little ones to grow up in, the team at HSE has put in careful thoughts to curate such a dedicated service to help you ensure that your home is ready for your children. Whether you are welcoming a newborn baby home or simply wish to do a thorough cleaning of your children’s room, this service was implemented just for you!

Are the cleaning solutions used safe?
As we only use eco-friendly cleaning solutions that are gentle, low in pH level, non-acidic and biodegradable, you may rest assured that our solutions used are much less harmful compared to the common ones used for household cleaning.


What is included in the Nursery Room Cleaning Service?

We clean and sanitize everything in your nursery room! From steam cleaning of your baby cot, the toys your children play with, to the curtains in the room – we do our best to eradicate hidden germs and bacteria trapped around the room.

*Should the baby sleep in another bed with the parents, we are also able to steam clean that particular bed
