Moving Out Cleaning



Moving Out Cleaning

A moving out cleaning usually refers to the cleaning of an empty apartment without any furniture. For this service, our professional cleaning crew will also clean the interior of your cabinets and wardrobes.

Should you be looking to have a thorough cleaning done before returning the apartment to your landlord after tenancy, this service is for you! Cleaning your home when moving out will also potentially increase the value of your property, making it look more presentable.
*Does not include post-renovation cleaning as part of its scope
