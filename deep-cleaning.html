Deep Cleaning



Deep Cleaning

Deep cleaning is more than just cleaning the surfaces and visible dirt, which involves thoroughly targeting hidden grime and bacteria. Our professional cleaning crew scrubs, sanitizes and cleans hard to reach areas for you, while focusing on improving the overall sanitization as well as living quality of your home and extending the lifespan of surfaces and appliances.

Deep Cleaning vs. Regular cleaning
Regular cleaning mainly involves typical home cleaning tasks conducted regularly to maintain the cleanliness of a house on a daily basis such as sweeping & mopping floors, wiping dust from surfaces and appliances, window frames, etc.

On the other hand, deep cleaning targets commonly neglected and hard-to-reach areas while including all regular cleaning tasks as well. This is typically done less frequent compared to regular cleaning.

How often should I deep clean?
Depending on the size of your home and number of occupants, it is generally recommended to conduct deep cleaning once every four to six months. However, certain areas of a home such as the kitchen and bathrooms are best recommended to be deep cleaned more frequently to keep the place fresh, hygienically safe and comfortable. This would also help maintaining the cleanliness an easier task.

Why should I deep clean my home?
Deep cleaning benefits you and your family members at home as it helps in maintaining hygiene, improving health and enhances the aesthetics. All these eventually leads to an improved productivity and instills positive emotional health in the household. As deep cleaning also prolongs the lifespan of the home’s surfaces and appliances, this means that durability in the long run will reduce the cost and need for any replacement.

When should I choose a deep cleaning service?
If you have not done a thorough cleaning for your home in a long time, it is the perfect time to do a deep cleaning now! Deep cleaning is suitable for those who are preparing for a special occasion, festive occasion or just simply wants to have a thorough cleaning to improve the hygiene and comfort of your living space.
